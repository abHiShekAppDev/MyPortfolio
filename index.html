RemindMe

It is an Android Based app which is basically made for teacher or student.With Help of this app a teacher/Student can easily maintain their scheduled just adding their scheduled one times and after that this app automatically remind the user just before 10 minutes the starting and ending of class time,so that they can easily attend them

• It also allow user to edit,update their classes according to their change in scheduled easily
• The most unique feature of this app is that if the user want to go on holiday then they can go by just clicking a button and they will be on vacation for the whole day means they will not notified about class in that day
